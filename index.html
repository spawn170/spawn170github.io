No housing? No car? NO PROBLEM!

New to D.C.?

Life in one of the most exciting cities in the world. It's easier, more affordable, and a lot more fun living where you can hop on the metro - to work, to get to a big event without traffic or parking hassles, and to meet friends across town.

Find a range of affordable housing options near every DC Metro station. Just click on each metro line to find a complete listing of available properties along that corridor, including exact distances from each station and rental contact information.

Live near the DC Metro. You'll never be far from home.

There are two ways to search for housing:

  1. Click the top tab corresponding with the color Metro line to view rental listings
  2. Click on the Metro line on the map to view nearby housing.
